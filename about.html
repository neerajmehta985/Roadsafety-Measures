About SafeRoads

Creating safer roads through awareness, education, and innovation.

Our Mission

We aim to reduce road accidents and fatalities by promoting safe driving habits, advocating for better infrastructure, and educating the public on road safety practices.

Our Vision

To build a world where everyone reaches their destination safely by driving change in road safety policies and behavior globally.

Key Awareness Programs

  • Drive Safe India: Nationwide school and college seminars on traffic rules.
  • Helmet Matters: Free distribution of helmets and safety kits.
  • Speed Alert: Campaign to raise awareness about speed limits in accident-prone zones.
