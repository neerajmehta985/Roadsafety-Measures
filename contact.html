Frequently Asked Questions

How can I volunteer for SafeRoads?

You can fill out the contact form with "Volunteer" in the subject. We'll get back to you with the next steps.

Do you offer school awareness programs?

Yes, we organize school campaigns and seminars. Contact us to schedule a session for your school.

How do I report unsafe road conditions?

Use our contact form or email us directly with pictures and location details. We collaborate with local authorities for action.
